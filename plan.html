Section 02 · The action plan

The Plan

Everything actionable from the report, stripped to what you'll actually do — organised by how often it happens, not by feature. No injectables or fillers; anything needing a clinic or prescription is grouped at the end and clearly flagged.

Filter by area

01

Daily foundation

Low effort · every day · the part that compounds
Morning skincareLow

Preserves youthful skin and protects against premature ageing — the single highest-leverage habit in the report.

When Every morningArea Skin

Gentle foaming cleanser → vitamin C serum → light moisturiser → factor-50 sunscreen as the final step.

Evening skincareLow

Smooths early texture, supports collagen and keeps pores clear, evening out tone over months.

When Every nightArea Skin

Cleanser → toner (combination skin) → retinol 0.25–0.5% (start 3 nights/wk) → niacinamide or HA moisturiser.

Eye careLow

Keeps an already-good under-eye area supported, softening the tear-trough and brightening pigment.

When DailyArea Eyes

Hydrating hyaluronic-acid eye serum daily; a vitamin C eye serum to brighten over time.

Lip careLow

Lips already suit the face; only basic upkeep is needed to keep them smooth.

When As neededArea Grooming

Apply a simple lip balm whenever they feel dry.

Sleep & hydrationLow

Good sleep and water keep the sclera clear and the eye region looking rested — the report ties both to a fresher gaze.

When DailyArea Lifestyle

Consistent sleep schedule; steady water intake through the day.

02

Weekly grooming & training

A little effort · on a rhythm · visible quickly
Haircut & side partMedium

Removes temple bulk and shifts visual weight onto the eyes and jaw; the side part breaks the vertical and adds a controlled wave.

When Every 3–4 weeksArea Hair

Shorter sides, length kept on top, styled into a structured side parting.

Sea-salt stylingLow

Defines your natural wave without stiffness, so the cut reads casual yet intentional.

When When stylingArea Hair

Light sea-salt spray, scrunched into the lengths while drying.

Beard shapingLow

A short, uniform beard along the mandibular border exposes the bone structure and sharpens the jaw angle.

When Weekly trimArea Grooming

Trim to a short even length; clean-shave the upper and mid-neck below the jaw line.

Eyebrow groomingLow

Cleans the outline while keeping the low, masculine shape — a firmer frame without looking over-groomed.

When Every 2–3 weeksArea Grooming

Thread the upper edge and outer tails only; a touch of dark brow gel through the mid-section.

Neck trainingMedium

Adds muscular thickness so the neck looks more solid and supports the jaw from every angle.

When 3× per weekArea Body

Neck extensions for the back, neck curls for the front — build volume gradually.

03

Ongoing recomposition

Patient · months-long · unlocks the mid-face
Gradual body-fat reductionMedium

Reveals your existing high cheekbones and lets the jawline read crisper — the report stresses gradual loss, not crash dieting, so the face doesn't look gaunt.

When OngoingArea Body

A gentle, sustained calorie deficit alongside training; aim for slow, steady progress.

Neck skin careLow

Minimises future lines on the neck as it becomes more defined.

When Nightly / dailyArea Skin

A gentle neck-specific retinol at night; daily sunscreen on the front and sides.

04

Optional — consult a professional

Non-invasive, but needs a clinic or prescription
Read first. None of these are injectables or surgery, but each involves an in-clinic treatment, a medicated product or a prescription. Treat them as ideas to raise with a qualified professional — not a self-prescription.
Whitening eye dropsConsult

Brighten the whites on tired days for a sharper, more rested look.

When OccasionalArea Eyes

Use sparingly and exactly as directed; pair with sleep and hydration.

Colour-correcting concealerOptional

Quietly hides the deepest under-eye shadow on busy days without obvious makeup.

When As wantedArea Eyes

A light peach-based corrector, patted only where the shadow is darkest.

Topical azelaic acidConsult

Reduces patchy pigmentation and post-inflammatory marks on the nose, cheeks and beard area.

When Short coursesArea Skin

Introduce alongside the base routine; confirm suitability with a professional.

IPL & Hydra FacialClinic

IPL evens colour and shrinks superficial vessels; Hydra Facials cleanse and hydrate with little downtime.

When PeriodicArea Skin

Book with a dermatologist or licensed clinic; not a home treatment.

Prescription pigment careRx

Under medical supervision, hydroquinone can reduce stubborn pigmentation.

When SupervisedArea Skin

Only on the advice of, and monitored by, a qualified doctor.
